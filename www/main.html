About ASU Book ME

ASU Book Me was created in November 2016 by Marete Kirimi, Dominique Dale, Dylan Lewis, Michealla Lindsey, and Santiago Lopez; members of Team Linq. The ASU Book ME app was created to help ASU students have one location to find out their professor’s availability. The application allows a student to book a time slot for a professor’s available office hours. The application will notify the professor by email that an appointment has been booked after the user confirms their selection.
